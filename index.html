I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science at Purdue University, advised by Professor Jeremiah Blocki. My research interests include security, data privacy, machine learning, and cryptography. In paticular, my current projects focus on proposing high-confidence statistical tools to rigorously analyze password online/offline attacks and analyzing the security of memory hard functions for passwod hashing.
